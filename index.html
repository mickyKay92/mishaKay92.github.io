Biography

Hello, my name is Michelle and welcome to my website. In this website, you will find a collection of my past commissions, personal creations and in-progress designs.
I have always had an interest in art and design. In school, I was always the one chosen to handle the design of a group excercise, and I was usually always top of the class whenever our work involved drawing or that required being technically-minded.

I always admired my uncle Marty, who is a professional graphic designer in Dundalk. I completed my work experience week for my A-Level Art in his office designing advertisements.
It was around this time that I started teaching myself how to use Adobe Photoshop in my spare time using a second-hand Wacom graphics tablet that my uncle gave me.

I quickly realised that I had a passion for digital painting, and I have recently branched into creating artworks on my new iPad Pro using the Procreate app.
